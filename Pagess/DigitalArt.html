Welcome!

Besides being a programmer, I’m also a digital artist. I’ve been creating digital art for quite along time, and I’m passionate about using digital tools to create stunning anime drawings.

You can see some of my projects here!!


Kamado Tanjiro from Kimetsu no yaiba

Expert in Anime Illustration & 3D Sculpting


My dedication to the art of anime character design is evident in my mastery of Clip Studio Paint and Ibis Paint X. These tools enable me to achieve the clean linework, expressive shading, and eye-catching color palettes characteristic of the anime style. My working knowledge of Photoshop and Illustrator further expands my capabilities for adding backgrounds, special effects, and polishing my artwork to professional standards. Additionally, my experience in Blender for 3D sculpting allows me to explore character design from multiple dimensions, enhancing my understanding of form and anatomy.
